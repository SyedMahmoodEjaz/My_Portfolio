Daraz clone websit design

I analyzed Daraz, Pakistan's #1 online shopping platform, through a 20-minute survey and family usability testing to identify user pain points. Key bad design practices include: "Cluttered Homepage Layout" with competing banners, "Poor Search Filter Accessibility," "Confusing Navigation Hierarchy," "Hidden Checkout Options" with inconsistent back button behavior, and "Overwhelming Product Listings" showing 20+ tiny product images per page. This real-world exercise revealed improvement opportunities like simpler layouts, better filters, and 6-8 larger product displays per page.
